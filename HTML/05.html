Seedhe Maut is a hip hop duo from Delhi, India, consisting of Siddhant Sharma (stage name Calm) and Abhijay Negi (stage name Encore ABJ). The two met at Spit Dope Inc., a Delhi underground battle rap league organized by MC Lavda and Encore ABJ.[1][2][3] In 2017, they signed to the independent music label Azadi Records.[4] In 2024, they founded their own label, DL91.[5]
